About Me

I am a project manager and entrepreneur with a Bachelor's degree in Business Project Management and Information Systems. I have experience working as a project manager for several years before founding my own company at the age of 22. At 25, I met the love of my life, a medical student. Faced with the choice between traveling with her for clinical studies and staying home to work on my business, I sought a career change that would accommodate our relationship. This allowed me to pursue my childhood passion for technology while applying the lessons I've learned in both academic and real-world business settings. My key skills include project management, information systems management, and networking. I hold the CompTIA A+, Network+, and Security+ certifications, demonstrating my knowledge in IT infrastructure, network administration, and cybersecurity. With a strong background in business project management and a passion for technology, I have a proven track record for generating tangible results. I am committed to leveraging my expertise and experience to drive effective project delivery and business growth.
