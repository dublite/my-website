About Me

Greg Curtis is a project manager and entrepreneur with a Bachelor's degree in Business Project Management and Information Systems. He has experience working as a project manager for several construction companies before founding his own construction company at the age of 23. Greg's key skills include project management, information systems management, and networking. He holds the CompTIA A+, Network+, and Security+ certifications, demonstrating his knowledge in IT infrastructure, network administration, and cybersecurity. With a strong background in business project management and a passion for entrepreneurship, Greg has successfully grown his construction company into a thriving venture. He is committed to leveraging his expertise and experience to drive effective project delivery and business growth.
